Our Services

A one-step gateway to start your career with, A platform to fulfil your dream with, A platform to get identified with- Gateways, a destination that enhances your practical learning and makes million of students industry-ready by providing relevant conceptual knowledge, top-class guidance and wide range of courses.

Career Explorations

Our professional experts provide beneficial advice for all your career-related queries with effective advice and unbiassed institute planning to help you explore various career opportunities for a successful future.

Finding Right fit Universities

Our dedicated counsellors will help you find career-defining courses among the list of top universities right from where to study & what to study.

Exam Preparations and Registration

We provide students with the best online and classroom coaching classes from the most experienced faculties for preparation of IELTS, PTE, Duolingo, GRE or SAT. We also help you to register for the exam & score the maximum with impeccable communication, superior interpersonal skills, credibility, transparency, passion & enthusiasm.

Admission Processing

Students often underestimate the time required to apply for admission to a university. At Gateways we ensure you make no mistakes by setting a schedule for you that begins well in advance of the time that you plan to begin your studies.

Immigration & Visa Services

We have a dedicated team of qualified immigration experts with expertise in the visa and immigration procedures of various countries to help you secure a student visa.

Express Service

Students will get free online coaching classes from certified faculties valid for 3 months, including guaranteed admission to any 1 university at your desired destination with providing maximum available scholarships & loan sanctions, followed by submission of Student Visa application within 24 hours or a full refund for our service.

Other Services

Our other services include education loan assistance, pre-departure orientation to post-landing services, from flight booking to pre departure orientation, forex assistance and accommodation.
